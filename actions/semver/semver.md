

## **ASD** ##

```yaml
mode: Mainline
branches:
  main:
    increment: None
major-version-bump-message: '^(?i)(breaking|major).*'
minor-version-bump-message: '^(?i)(feat|minor).*'
patch-version-bump-message: '^(?i)(fix|patch).*'
tag-prefix: '[vV]'
commit-message-incrementing: Enabled
commit-date-format: yyyy-MM-dd

```
